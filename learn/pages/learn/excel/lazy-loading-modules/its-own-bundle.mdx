## Their own bundle

As you can see, firebase modules have their own bundle and its name looks like:

```
static/chunks/[a-random-string].js
```

![](/learn/static/images/llm-analyzer2.jpg)

This bundle is loaded when you try to import `firebase/app` and `firebase/database` modules.
